the Villas

SK PLACE is a luxury development of three very spacious independent Villas overlooking the sea on the edge of the pretty fishing village of Almyrida with breathtaking views across the Aegean towards Souda Bay and magnificent panoramic views of the White Mountains. Each villa reflects a different architectural approach with either traditional Cretan (Villa Anemos), island style (Villa Ammos) or modern - minimal (Almyra Residence) characteristics.

 

almyra Residence

 

Villa anemos

 

Villa ammos

The stylish interior decor combines simple elegance with subtle accents of comfort and luxury. Enjoy the most wonderful restful sleep in superior quality Swedish beds from "Dux", relax on sumptuous sofas or dine out under the stars and have peace of mind with easy to use electronic control systems for satellite TV, music and air conditioning..

 

 

The interior spaces flow seamlessly to the outdoor areas through the extensive use of glass, allowing the spectacular scenery to be embraced from inside or out. Whilst the lush colourful gardens of bougainvillea, citrus trees, laurels, jasmine and herbs diffuse their irresistible perfumes, be transported into the abyss of endless blue from your swimming pool overlooking the sea and beyond to where the horizon meets the sky. The wonderful atmosphere of deepest light blue, blissful sunsets and magical starry skies create the perfect scene for escaping everyday routine. Awaken your senses and experience the delight of fresh clean sea water, invigorating warm sunshine, cool mountain air and soft golden sand.

 

From the intimate atmosphere to the luxurious surroundings brimming with character and charm, we have thought of everything to make your stay unforgettable
